A Church home for Cincinnati Families

If you live in the heart Cincinnati, OH area you can find a church home where you are loved and can grow in the faith. At Hartwell Baptist Church we love Jesus, love the Bible and love people.

At Hartwell Baptist Church we have one statement that we repeat often: It does make a difference what you believe. Opinions, religious tradition, and fuzzy feelings all have their place, but not when it comes to eternal truths. Jesus stated in John 14:6, I am the way, THE TRUTH, and the life; no man cometh to the Father but by me.

What if you could find?

Truth

Whether it is our Sunday School, our Morning or Evening Preaching times, or our Wednesday Evening Bible study and prayer- we use the Bible to discover truth.

Grace

Learn about what Jesus has done for you. He’s paved the way for you to have a meaningful relationship with God and an eternal home in heaven.

Purpose

Live for more than toys and a paycheck. Figure out what God made you. Be a part of something with eternal consequences.

Community

What are services like?

At Hartwell Baptist Church we experience Church like it used to be. We sing enthusiastically songs that praise the Lord and prepare our hearts for the preaching of the Word of God. Our Pastor preaches in a way that is both easily understood and expositional in content. Each service is brought to a conclusion by receiving an offering.

What about an offering?

The offering is a way for God’s people to obey the Word of God through giving to meet the needs of Hartwell Baptist Church.

When do you meet?

Sundays:

Sunday School - 9:30AM
Morning Service - 10:30AM
Evening Service - 6:00 PM

Wednesdays

Bible Study & Prayer Meeting - 7:00 PM

Is there anything for my kids?

We provide the following for children:

  • A Sunday school service for elementary aged kids.
  • Occasional youth activities for teenagers

Am I expected to give?

No. We do take an offering every service, but it is for our members. We do not expect our guests to give anything. You’ve given us enough by visiting.

What do you believe?

We believe what Baptist people have always believed including the inspiration and inerrancy of Scripture, the Trinity of the godhead, the Virgin Birth of Christ, Salvation by grace through Faith in Christ’s finished work, the premillennial return of Christ, etc.
